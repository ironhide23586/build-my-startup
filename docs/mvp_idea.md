# MVP Idea

Generated: 2025-10-30 01:26:03

Name: "Dietary Pal" 

Description: An application designed to provide quick and easy nutritional information for common foods and ingredients, helping users to maintain a balanced diet. 

Features: 
- Input any food item to get a detailed breakdown of its nutritional content (carbs, proteins, fats, vitamins, etc.) 
- Option to store and track daily nutritional intake
- Provides healthy food suggestions based on user's dietary preferences and nutritional needs
- Ability to scan barcodes to quickly identify the nutritional content of packaged foods
- Reminder feature to encourage regular meal and water intake 

Tech: Python, Flask, MySQL, Nutritionix API
